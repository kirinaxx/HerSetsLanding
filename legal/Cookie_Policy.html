This Cookie Policy explains how HerSets uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website and within the app.

1. What Are Cookies and Similar Technologies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website. In our mobile app, we use comparable technologies — such as mobile identifiers (IDFA/GAID), SDKs, and local storage — that work similarly to cookies to remember your preferences and understand app usage.

2. Types We Use

TypePurposeCan you opt out?
EssentialKeep you logged in, remember session and security settingsNo — required for the app/site to function
AnalyticsUnderstand how features are used so we can improve HerSetsYes, via device settings or in-app preferences
PreferencesRemember settings like theme, saved filters, or regionYes, though some features may reset
Advertising / attributionMeasure the performance of marketing campaigns that brought you to HerSetsYes, via device-level ad tracking controls

3. On Our Website

If you visit hersets.com, we and our service providers may use cookies for the same purposes above, including analytics tools that help us understand site traffic. Where required by law, we'll show a cookie consent banner allowing you to accept or manage non-essential cookies before they're set.

4. In the Mobile App

On iOS, app tracking (such as IDFA-based advertising identifiers) is subject to Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework — you'll be prompted to allow or deny tracking, and you can change this anytime in your device's Settings → Privacy → Tracking. On Android, you can reset or opt out of your advertising ID under your device's Google Settings → Ads.

5. How to Manage Your Preferences

  • Browser cookies: Most browsers let you block or delete cookies through their settings menu.
  • Mobile tracking: Adjust ad tracking permissions in your device's system settings, as described above.
  • In-app analytics: Where available, toggle analytics sharing under Profile → Settings → Privacy.

Note that blocking essential cookies or identifiers may affect core functionality, like staying logged in.

6. Third-Party Tools

We may use third-party analytics and attribution providers (for example, product analytics or crash-reporting SDKs) that set their own cookies or identifiers under their respective privacy policies. See our Privacy Policy for more on how we work with service providers.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our tools or practices change. We'll update the "Last updated" date above when we do.
