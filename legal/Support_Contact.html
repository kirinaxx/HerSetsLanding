What to Include in Your Message

  • The email address associated with your HerSets account
  • Your device type and app version (found under Profile → Settings → About)
  • A short description of the issue, and screenshots if helpful

Common Topics We Can Help With

Account & login issues

Trouble signing in, resetting your password, or accessing your account.

Billing & subscriptions

Questions about a charge, subscription status, or how to cancel. Since purchases go through the App Store or Google Play, refund requests are ultimately handled by Apple or Google — but we're happy to help you sort out what happened.

Bugs & technical issues

Something not working as expected? Let us know what you were doing when it happened.

Account deletion & data requests

See our Account Deletion Policy, or email us directly to request your data or delete your account.

Feedback & feature requests

We read every message — tell us what you'd love to see in HerSets.

Report content or a user

If you've encountered content that violates our Terms of Service, email us with a link or screenshot and we'll review it promptly.
