Your client details are everywhere.

Camera rolls. Paper notebooks. Random notes apps. Booking platforms with a tiny box. Text threads. Memory. For too long, beauty technicians have been forced to keep the most important details of their work scattered across all of it.

HerSets
One organized place. Built for how you work.

What's inside

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

01
Client Profiles

Every preference, sensitivity, service history, and note — organized by client, searchable in seconds.

02
Lash Maps

Digital lash maps with curl, style, diameter, and adhesive notes per eye zone. Save templates, build on them each visit.

03
Nail Formulas

Shape, gel type, color codes, brand, finish. Track nail designs per visit so you can replicate exactly.

04
Today's Schedule

Your day at a glance — each appointment shows the client's last service, alerts, and key preferences upfront.

05
Alerts & Flags

Mark sensitivities, allergies, patch test results that surface automatically before every appointment.

06
Visit History

A full timeline of every service — fills, sets, changes, notes. Your client's story, always at your fingertips.
