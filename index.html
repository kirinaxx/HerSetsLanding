Built-in templates

Five templates. Ready on day one.

No setup, no configuration. Open the app and your templates are waiting — built specifically around how lash and nail artists actually work.

01
Client Profile

Everything about a client in one place. Name, contact, skin type, eye shape, allergies, patch test results, and personal notes. The profile updates automatically after every visit so it always reflects who your client is today.

Contact Allergies Patch test Eye shape Notes
02
Service Maps

Visual diagrams built for each discipline. Lash artists get a 5-zone eye map with curl, length and diameter per zone. Hair stylists get a top-down head map for color placement — crown, front, sides, nape, money piece. Estheticians get a face analysis map with zones for forehead, cheeks, T-zone and chin. Every discipline gets a diagram that matches how you actually think about the work.

Lash eye map Hair head map Skin face map Extension row map
03
Service Formulas

Every discipline has its own formula fields. Nail: per-finger color code, gel type, brand, finish, add-ons. Hair color: root / mid-length / toner formulas with brand, shade, developer and timing. Hair extensions: color blend (multiple shades), brand, length, weight. Esthetician: peel percentage, LED settings, active ingredients. The fields match the service — not a generic notes box.

Nail formula Hair color formula Extension blend Skin treatment params
04
Visit Notes

A per-appointment record of what happened at each session — service type, products used, retention observations, client feedback, and anything worth remembering. Builds into a full timeline you can scroll through before any appointment.

Service type Products used Retention Client feedback
05
Weekly Schedule

A color-coded weekly view of all your appointments — sorted by service type, duration, and client. See lash fills, nail sets, and new clients all at a glance. Tap any slot to pull up the client's full profile before they arrive.

Day & time Service Duration Client name Color tag
